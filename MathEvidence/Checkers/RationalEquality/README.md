# RationalEquality checker

## Claim

Establishes `eval lhs = eval rhs` over `ℚ` for the RFC 0001 rational-expression
fragment, under explicit nonzero conditions on certificate denominator factors.

## Assurance

- Mode: `kernel_replay` (decidable multivariate polynomial identity + Lean soundness).
- Backends are untrusted generators only; they never supply a trusted Boolean.

## Algorithm

1. Bind certificate `requestDigest` to the request digest.
2. Reject ill-formed expressions / factors.
3. Prove numerator identity of `lhs - rhs` after common-denominator construction
   via sparse polynomial equality (`polyEqual` / `combineLike`).
4. Require certificate factors to cover every `div` (and rational-literal)
   denominator subexpression in `lhs` and `rhs`.
5. Emit equality under definedness; never claim identity at poles.

## Limitations

- Does not totalize division at zeros.
- Does not assert minimality of the condition set (redundant factors allowed).
- Meta-level reification from arbitrary Lean terms is owned by `MathEvidence.Tactic`.

## Offline replay

`Replay.lean` reruns `check` with no adapter invocation.

Committed SymPy and Mathematica evidence under `evidence/` is mirrored into
`OfflineFixtures.lean` (generated by `scripts/generate_lean_offline_fixtures.py`)
and discharged with `native_decide`. Hand-written unit fixtures live in `Tests.lean`.

Wire JSON decoding (name-based AST → indexed `Expr`) is in `Decode.lean` /
`IR/RationalExpr/Wire.lean` for tactic and tooling use.
